Jamb CBT Centre @ Blue Horizon Schools

Blue Horizon Schools is officially approved as a JAMB CBT Centre, with a capacity to accommodate 277 candidates.

Candidates can now complete their JAMB registration and examinations in a secure and well-equipped environment. Registration is open to both Blue Horizon Schools students and external candidates.

For registration details, requirements, and enquiries, please contact the school or visit the school campus.

Established in 2024, the BHS is duly registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 1990, with the Corporate Affairs Commission. Its location is unique, because of the serenity of the environment, the ease with which it can be accessed, and the spacious and secure facilities in the fastest-growing urban parts of Kaduna Metropolis.
